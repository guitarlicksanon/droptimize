## Full Profile Overview

**Web Optimization for Local Businesses | Plain HTML/CSS/JS | Cloudflare Pages | No Platform Lock-In**

---

I rebuild local business websites to hit 100/100 SEO and 100/100 Security - verified scores, not estimates.

Most local business websites look fine on the surface but fail on the things Google actually measures: structured data, security headers, canonical URLs, schema, and mobile performance. The result is a site that looks professional but ranks behind competitors that have these fundamentals right.

I fix that from the ground up.

---

**What I build:**

Every rebuild uses plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript deployed to Cloudflare Pages. No WordPress. No Wix. No page builders. No monthly platform fees. You own the files. You can take them anywhere.

Every build ships with:

- **100/100 SEO:** Title tag, meta description, H1/H2 optimization, canonical URL, Open Graph (og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url), Twitter Card, JSON-LD structured data schema (correct @type per industry), sitemap.xml, robots.txt, image alt attributes, viewport meta tag, page speed signals
- **100/100 Security:** HTTPS enforced, HSTS (max-age 2yr + includeSubDomains + preload), Content-Security-Policy, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options, Referrer-Policy, Permissions-Policy, Cross-Origin-Opener-Policy, Cross-Origin-Resource-Policy
- **Admin panel:** Non-technical owner can update hours, post specials, manage service areas, toggle emergency closure - no code required
- **SureCritic integration:** Review widget slots built into every page - activation is copy-paste when ready
- **Mobile responsive:** Tested at 375px, 768px, and 1024px

**Why not WordPress?**

WordPress requires ongoing plugin updates, security patches, hosting plan renewals, and page builder subscriptions. Most local businesses don't need any of that. A plain HTML site on Cloudflare Pages loads faster, is harder to hack, costs ~$12/year (domain only), and scores better on every technical audit.

## Upwork Notes

**Portfolio:** Add the Colin Auto Repair build as a portfolio item with before/after audit scores. Attach a screenshot of the `curl` header output showing all 9 security headers present.

**Proposal approach:** When applying to "redesign my website" or "fix my SEO" jobs for local businesses, open with the audit. Run their site through the checklist, paste in their actual scores, and show the gap. A client who sees "Your current Security score: 12/100 - here's every missing header" is converted. Don't pitch the solution before showing the diagnosis.

**Fixed-price vs hourly:** Lead with fixed-price packages for clarity. Hourly for complex integrations or ongoing retainers. Clients with budget uncertainty prefer fixed-price - remove friction.

**Connects budget:** Prioritize jobs posted in the last 24 hours. After 48 hours, competition spikes. Set up saved searches for: "website redesign local business", "SEO fix local", "Cloudflare Pages", "WordPress to static".
