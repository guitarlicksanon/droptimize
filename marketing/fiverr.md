## Notes on Fiverr Settings

**Search tags matter more than category.** The tags on Gig 1 should reflect what buyers actually type - "local business website" and "website rebuild" get more search volume from the right buyers than generic "web development."

**Pricing anchor:** The $49 audit is the funnel. A buyer who orders the audit and sees a 38/100 Security score is primed to order the $299-$749 rebuild. Consider offering a $30 audit credit toward any rebuild ordered within 14 days.

**Gig images:** Use before/after format - left side shows a bad audit score screenshot, right side shows 100/100. Simple, visual, immediately communicates the value.

**Response time:** Keep under 2 hours. Fiverr's algorithm surfaces gigs with fast response time. Set up mobile notifications.

**Buyer requirements:** Ask these before the clock starts. Waiting on a logo file after you've already started loses you 12-24 hours on a 5-day order. Collect everything upfront.
